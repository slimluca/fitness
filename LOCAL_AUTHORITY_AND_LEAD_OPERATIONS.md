## Review collection workflow

Ask for reviews after:

- a clear early win in the first 2 to 4 weeks
- a completed short package
- a visible result milestone
- a successful villa or travel-coaching experience

When requesting a review, ask for:

- first name or initials permission
- service used
- what they wanted help with
- what changed
- whether the quote can appear on the website
- whether a Google review is also approved

If the client wants privacy:

- use initials, first name only, or `Name withheld by request`
- keep location broad if needed
- avoid publishing exact dates, exact body metrics, or images without written approval

## Review request prompt

Suggested message:

`Thank you again for training with Fitness Grand Baie. If the coaching has helped, I would really appreciate a short review about your experience, what you wanted help with, and what changed for you. If you prefer, I can also share it on the website in a privacy-safe format using initials only.`
