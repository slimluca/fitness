## 2. First Reviews To Add

1. Homepage: 2 to 3 short reviews about privacy, professionalism, and consistency
2. `/personal-trainer-mauritius`: 1 Mauritius-facing review and 1 Grand Baie-specific review
3. `/services/private-personal-training`: 2 reviews about one-to-one coaching and the private environment
4. `/pricing`: 1 review that justifies the premium investment
5. `/results`: strongest longer-form quote or measurable story
6. `/contact` and `/about`: short reassurance-focused reviews

- `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL`

- `Request Availability`

## 6. First Quick Wins To Look For

- Homepage gets impressions but weak clicks: improve title, meta description, H1, and first-screen trust.
- Pricing gets visits but weak action: improve reassurance, proof, and "best next step" guidance near pricing cards.
- WhatsApp clicks are strong but forms are weak: keep WhatsApp prominent and reduce form hesitation.
- Blog traffic grows but lead quality is weak: tighten article-to-service links and end-of-article CTA specificity.

## 7. First Lead-Quality Signals To Watch

Higher-intent signs:

- clear service preference
- realistic start timing
- schedule details
- WhatsApp-friendly follow-up
- clear goal or challenge

Lower-intent signs:

- vague goals
- no timing clues
- no preferred service
- no reply preference

## 8. First Search Console Patterns To Check

- homepage impressions
- Mauritius landing-page impressions and CTR
- Grand Baie landing-page impressions and CTR
- service-page queries
- pricing-page queries
- blog posts getting impressions but weak business impact

If one page earns impressions but weak clicks, improve the snippet and first screen before changing the rest of the page.
