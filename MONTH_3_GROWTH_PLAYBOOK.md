# Month-3 Growth Playbook

Use this note once Fitness Grand Baie has real impressions, clicks, and the first leads coming in consistently.

## 1. What To Review Every Week

- `GA4`: sessions, top landing pages, thank-you visits, WhatsApp clicks, consultation clicks, request-availability clicks, form submissions, and blog CTA clicks.
- `Search Console`: impressions, clicks, CTR, average position, top queries, and pages gaining impressions but not enough clicks yet.
- `Forms`: lead quality, response speed, and whether enquiries are coming from homepage, pricing, service pages, landing pages, or blog articles.

## 2. Pages To Optimize First

Watch these before touching the rest of the site:

1. `/`
2. `/personal-trainer-mauritius`
3. `/personal-trainer-grand-baie`
4. `/services/private-personal-training`
5. `/services/online-coaching`
6. `/pricing`
7. `/contact`
8. `/book-consultation`
9. `/results`

## 3. Search Console-Led Quick Wins

- `High impressions, low CTR`: improve the title tag, meta description, H1, and first-screen trust message before rewriting the whole page.
- `Page 2 rankings`: add stronger internal links from homepage, relevant service pages, top category pages, and related blog articles.
- `Article impressions, weak clicks`: improve article title clarity, first paragraph quality, and the promise made in the search snippet.
- `Clicks but weak conversions`: strengthen the first CTA row, proof near the top, and reassurance about privacy, appointments, and response time.
- `Traffic but weak lead quality`: make the ideal client fit clearer and add more guidance around which service or next step to choose.

## 4. How To Read Lead Patterns

- `Homepage-origin leads`: usually broad-intent. If weak, clarify who the business is best for sooner.
- `Pricing-origin leads`: usually higher intent. If weak, improve service-fit guidance and what happens after enquiry.
- `Service-page leads`: usually warmer. If weak, add stronger proof or clearer availability guidance near the top.
- `Landing-page leads`: often comparison traffic. If weak, sharpen local trust and the best next move.
- `Blog-origin leads`: often earlier-stage. If weak, improve article-to-service mapping and end-of-article CTAs.
- `WhatsApp-heavy leads`: often urgency or convenience driven. Protect speed and clarity.
- `Form-heavy leads`: often more thoughtful and higher-context. Use those leads to improve qualification copy and follow-up quality.

## 5. First Proof To Insert

Replace real proof in this order:

1. Homepage: 2 to 3 short privacy-safe reviews about professionalism, private-gym comfort, and consistency.
2. `/personal-trainer-mauritius`: 1 broader Mauritius-facing review and 1 Grand Baie-specific private-coaching review.
3. `/services/private-personal-training`: 2 reviews that explain why one-to-one private coaching felt better than a public gym.
4. `/pricing`: 1 review that makes the investment feel justified through coaching quality and structure.
5. `/results`: the strongest longer-form quote or best measurable story.
6. `/contact` and `/about`: short reviews that reinforce professionalism, privacy, and trust.

## 6. First Real Assets To Insert

Replace assets in this order:

1. Trainer portrait
2. Homepage hero or private-studio image
3. About-page trust image
4. Results or approved progress proof image
5. Credentials or certification visual
6. Private personal training service image
7. Online coaching image
8. Contact or consultation reassurance image

## 7. CTA Checks To Make First

Watch these first:

- `Book Consultation` from homepage, pricing, Mauritius landing page, and private personal training page
- `WhatsApp Now` from homepage, contact, pricing, and blog articles
- `Request Availability` from service pages, contact, and landing pages

If one CTA clearly wins:

- keep the winner prominent
- improve the weaker path instead of removing it immediately
- check whether the weaker path has lower trust or more friction, not just lower clicks

## 8. Blog-To-Business Checks

- Make sure top articles link to the most relevant service or landing page.
- Keep category pages pointing into services, pricing, consultation, and results.
- If blog traffic rises but leads stay weak, improve end-of-article CTA copy before publishing more content.
- If readers stay in the archive too long, strengthen "start here" and "best next step" pathways.

## 10. Best Month-3 Quick Wins

- Improve the CTR of pages already earning impressions before creating more pages.

- Use live lead-source patterns to decide which CTA or page gets refined next.
